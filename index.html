Why Reading ?

Reading is a very useful habit that you can do to raise your knowledge, and consume more knowledge which increases your intelligence.

Advantages of Reading:

Brain Power and Mental Health:

  • Strengthen Neural Path Ways: Reading is quite literally a workout for your brain. it simulates complex networks of circuits and signals.

  • Reduces stress: Getting completely absorbed in reading a story lowers your heart rate and eases muscle tension. studies have shown that it can reduce stress by 68%

  • Rebuilds your Attention Span: in a world of fast paced, 15-second videos, reading forces your brain to concentrate on one single task for a sustained period.

Personal Growth and Intelligence:

  • Expands your vocabulary: Books expose you to words, phrases, and contexts you rarely face in daily conversation. This naturally improves your ability to express your thoughts.

  • Offers a good Investment: For a few dollars to buy a book or totally for free in libraries, you gain access to knowledge, life experiences, and imagination of someone else.

  • Increases empathy: When you read fiction, your brain starts to react as if you are experiencing the events yourself. Stepping into perspectives of different characters improves your emotional intelligence and ability to understand others.
